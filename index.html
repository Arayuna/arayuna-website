The Green Republic of Arayuna is located in the Esequibo region, an area situated between Guyana and Venezuela. This region is known for its geographical diversity and rich natural heritage.

Commitment to the Jungle and its Guardians

In Arayuna, we recognize that indigenous peoples have been the true guardians of the Amazon for millennia. Our project is not only a green nation, but also a pact with those who have lived in harmony with the Earth.

A portion of our resources will be directly allocated to support indigenous communities in vulnerable situations, strengthening their autonomy, culture, and well-being.

Furthermore, the Green Republic of Arayuna actively commits to the comprehensive protection of the Amazon, rejecting any form of deforestation, destructive extractivism, or forced displacement.

Arayuna is born to care for what still lives and to regenerate what has been damaged.
